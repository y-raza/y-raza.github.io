TriFusion Solutions is a boutique federal IT management consulting firm headquartered in Fairfax, Virginia. We specialize in the Department of Homeland Security enterprise — where the principal you hire is the one who shows up.

Real P&L accountability, senior client relationships, and domain expertise at the executive level — that is what TriFusion brings. At TriFusion, the principal you hire is the one who does the work. The partner you engage is the partner who shows up.
